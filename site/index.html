Addressable LED control protocol

Move pixels.
Not complexity.

Opalinx is a compact, implementation-independent binary protocol connecting software to reliable, high-performance LED controllers.

Read the specification Open Workbench

A small, firm foundation.

The core does the necessary work—framing, discovery, configuration, pixel transfer, coordinated output, and recovery—without prescribing firmware architecture.

01

Clear wire contract

COBS framing, CRC protection, deterministic errors, and transaction correlation over reliable byte streams.

02

Frame-coherent output

Buffered RGB and RGBW data with coordinated multi-channel Show operations and a deliberately small pipeline.

03

Room to expand

Stable information records and namespaced vendor extensions preserve space without burdening the baseline.

Runs in your browser

Configure. Test. Diagnose.

Opalinx Workbench connects directly to compatible USB controllers through Web Serial. No installation is required in supported Chromium browsers.
